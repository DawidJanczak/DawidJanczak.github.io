Before you begin

Trail entrance

The entrance is in Baliwan (八里灣), located in Fengbin Village (豐濱村). There is a big parking lot here, right at the entrance. The hike starts by crossing a river, don't attempt this after a typhoon or otherwise rainy period, it might be difficult to cross.

Transportation

As usual car or scooter is the best option. If you don't mind running on the road before and after the hike you could technically run from the main road in Fengbin, around 8km away from the trail entrance. At the moment of writing there are three buses going there from Hualian Station (花蓮車站), those are 1140, 1140A and 1145. There is also bus 1125 from Guangfu Train Station (光復車站). It's a shorter route, but the buses aren't frequent. This is the bus stop you want, there is a 7-11 and a few restaurants around as well.

The Hike

From the parking lot climb the embankment, go down to the river and cross it. There are stairs on the other side to your left, make your way there and climb up. From here on it's mostly climb with a bit of flat terrain around
